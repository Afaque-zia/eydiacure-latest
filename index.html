Profession a will for voluntary compliance

EydiaCure Pvt Ltd has been doing an enhanced practical programme on regulations and training for our members to raise awareness of continuous compliance for a long time. This is to make sure that relevant regulations, such as the fair trade act, anti-graft law, and pharmaceutical affairs act, are followed. Even though we try to manage in a way that follows the law and adapts to changes in the outside world, such as changes in government policy and new rules, if you break the law by accident, it can hurt not only you but also our company and customers.

Ethical and Regulation Compliance Standard

All executives and workers of EydiaCure Pvt Ltd are expected to put the needs of their clients first in all aspects of their work, including listening to their feedback, accepting their word when it comes to what they want, and acting accordingly.

Compliance with Laws and Regulations

Executives and employees of EydiaCure Pvt Ltd are required to follow not only this Standard but also all applicable Indian laws (such as the Fair Trade Act, Pharmacy Act, Pharmaceutical Safety Regulation, Commercial Code, Tax Code, Industrial Safety and Health Act, Wastes Control Act, and others), regulations, company regulations, and ethical standards.

Gifts and Entertainment

The management and staff at EydiaCure Pvt Ltd are not obligated to accept or provide any gifts or entertainment of the types listed below in the course of conducting business. Providing gifts, entertainment, or special treatment with the goal of influencing product-related business decisions. Providing gifts, entertainment, or special treatment throughout the current purchase or contract decision-making process. All monetary presents, such as “Gift Certificates.”

Environment, Prodhealth and safety

In all business activities, the executives and employees of EydiaCure Pvt Ltd shall engage in ways that ensure the safety of oneself and others, or methods that comply with the conditions of the relevant environment, health, and safety.
